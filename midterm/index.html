Our Story

Established in 2022, Ethiopia Restaurant adds a distinctly modern flair to Berkeley's rich culinary landscape. Our intimate restaurant encourages communal dining under hanging tapestries and warm pendant lighting. Whether you're a local or a visitor, we welcome you to discover a new favorite cuisine and dining destination. We promise an unforgettable cultural journey with every bite
